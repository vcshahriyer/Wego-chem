ABOUT WEGO CHEMICAL GROUP

Wego Chemical Group is a global supplier, importer, and distributor of chemicals, minerals, and raw materials. We provide supply solutions for a variety of chemical products with diverse applications and uses, servicing a wide range of industries. Our team has been operating for over 35 years, with offices in four continents and consistent business in over 25 countries. The Wego team has the expertise and the track record needed to provide exceptional and reliable chemical supply solutions for its customers and partners
